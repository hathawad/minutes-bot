# Kendall Christian School (KCS)

## Overview
Kendall Christian School is a private, PCA-affiliated (Presbyterian Church in America) Christian school located in Miami, Florida. Founded in **1973** as a ministry of Kendall Presbyterian Church, KCS has served the Kendall community for over 50 years.

## Mission
*"Motivate the next generation of independent Christian thinkers, prepare life-long learners, and equip students with Biblical truths."*

## Accreditation
KCS is accredited by [FCIS (Florida Council of Independent Schools)](https://www.fcis.org/), one of only 10 state associations nationally recognized by NAIS. The school undergoes a comprehensive accreditation review every 5-6 years.
